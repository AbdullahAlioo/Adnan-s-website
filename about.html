About Us

Learn more about who we are and what we stand for

TarQeem is a non-profit platform dedicated to the preservation and promotion of Urdu literature, language, and culture. We believe that Urdu is not just a language, but a reflection of centuries of history, tradition, and artistic expression. Our mission is to bring people together across borders and backgrounds through meaningful literary and cultural events. By hosting mushairas, symposiums, workshops, and community gatherings, we aim to highlight the richness of our shared heritage and inspire a deeper

appreciation for the beauty, eloquence, and universality of the Urdu language. Through our initiatives, TarQeem strives to create a vibrant platform where established and emerging voices can be heard, celebrated, and passed on to future generations.

Our Core Values

Founder

Adnan Munawar

Founder

Adnan Munawar is a distinguished poet and literary figure, passionately committed to the promotion of Urdu literature and culture in the United Arab Emirates. With a deep appreciation for the artistic and historical value of the Urdu language, he has dedicated his work to creating meaningful platforms for poets, writers, and literature enthusiasts. His debut poetry collection (Book), Tashbeeh, was published by Bazm-e-Urdu Dubai and formally inaugurated at the Sharjah International Book Fair 2024, earning him recognition for his eloquence, depth, and unique poetic voice. Adnan is widely respected in literary circles across the world for his ability to bridge cultural and geographical divides through the art of words.
