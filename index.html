TARQEEM INTERNATIONAL MUSHAIRA 2025 – Pakistan Chapter

Venue: Punjab Institute of Language, Art & Culture (PILAC), Lahore Date: 5 October 2025

The Tarqeem International Mushaira 2025 at PILAC, Lahore, was a grand celebration of Urdu poetry and literary brilliance. Presided over by Shahid Zaki (Sialkot), the event featured Malik Irfan (Dubai) as Guest of Honour and Shabbir Hassan (Lahore) as Guest of Distinction.

The evening began with a graceful welcome by Maryam Shahzadi and was beautifully compered by Shadab Abbasi, whose engaging style kept the audience captivated.

Prominent poets — Ammar Iqbal, Sabahat Urooj, Soheel Sani, Shahbaz Nayyar, Raaz Ehtesham, Fayyaz Bostan, Aarb Hashmi, Tajammul Kazmi, Ahmed Abdullah, and Usama Andaleeb — presented their best work to an appreciative Lahore audience.

Hosted by Adnan Munawar and Ahmed Ali Kaif, the event stood out for its seamless organization, vibrant participation, and poetic excellence — a memorable addition to Lahore’s rich literary tradition.
