Highlights 2024

International Urdu Poetry Symposium (Mushaira) in Sharjah held by TARQEEM
in titled
'Hum Naghma Sraa Kuch Ghazlou'n Ke '
An International Mushaira was held at Pakistan Social Center Sharjah on Sunday,
19th May 2024 The event was organised by Tarqeem, in collaboration with
Chai Shai Restaurant and Cafe. The mushairah set a precedent, as this was Tarqeem's
first international mushairah and was attended by renowned poets from India, Pakistan
and the UAE. Presided by poet, writer and critic, Akhter Usman and attended by
Dr. Sabahat Asim Wasti, Syed Sarosh Asif, Naveen Joshi, Ashwani Mittal,
Dilawar Ali Aazar, Ali Zeerak, Ehya Bhojpuri, Nilofer Afzal, Usman Habeeb, Ahmed
Jahangeer, Malik Irfan, Abrar Umar and Muskan Syed Riaz
The event concluded on a high note, with the audience visibly moved and
enriched — a true testament to the enduring power of Urdu poetry. Tarqeem successfully
set a new standard, not just for literary gatherings in the UAE, but for international poetic discourse.
