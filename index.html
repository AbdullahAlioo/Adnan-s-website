TarQeem International Mushaira 2025

Tarqeem UAE, in collaboration with Sharaf Exchange, hosted a truly memorable evening where the magic of words captivated every heart.

Held on 23rd August 2025 at the Pakistan Social Centre Sharjah, the grand literary gathering brought together renowned poets from the USA, Canada, Qatar, India, and Pakistan, who enthralled the audience with their soulful verses. The evening was further graced by the presence of leading businessmen and CEOs from across the UAE, adding prestige to the occasion.

The distinguished guests of honor were Dr. Sabahat Asim Wasiti and Mr. Shahabuddin, whose presence added immense value to the event. During the ceremony, eminent poets were presented with honorary shields along with Ajrak, a cultural symbol of Saraiki heritage, as a token of appreciation.

The Mushaira not only stood as a shining example of promoting Urdu literature but also carried a powerful message of cultural harmony and community unity.
